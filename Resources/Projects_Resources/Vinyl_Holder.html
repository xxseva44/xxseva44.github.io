Mounting Made Easy.
With a focus on elegance, ease of use and a tame design; this easy to use vinyl wall mount solution allows for a low profile, secure mount all while allowing the release of the vinyl with a pull of the tab. Being composed of 2 corner pieces, they simply mount opposite to eacher other.
Lets Take A Closer Look.
Being a low profile mount, this is unlike other "mounts" that require the vinyl sleeve to simply sit on what is essentially a ledge; users will have a hard time trying to knock sleeves off the wall with this mount. Further more, it does not reqire much additional space to position sleeves into the mount, allowing for a densely packed display wall.
